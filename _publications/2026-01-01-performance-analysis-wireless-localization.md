This paper studies wireless localization using received signal strength and angle-of-arrival measurements from randomly deployed sensors. It develops performance expressions and bounds for finite Poisson sensor networks, and examines when those results remain reliable for broader classes of spatial sensor deployments.

The manuscript is currently under review. [Read the preprint on arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.06396).
